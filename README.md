## 2. The FlirtIQ Keyboard Extension

FlirtIQ includes an **optional** iOS keyboard extension. The main app works fully without it — you only need the keyboard if you want AI-powered reply suggestions directly from your keyboard inside other apps.

### Full Access

To generate AI suggestions, the keyboard requires iOS's **"Allow Full Access"** permission, which you must enable manually. Full Access allows the keyboard to make network requests to our servers. You can revoke it at any time:

> Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → FlirtIQ → Allow Full Access

### What the keyboard sends to our servers

- **Only when you explicitly tap a FlirtIQ action:** the text you selected or the current conversation context, plus your anonymous user ID.
- This text is sent over HTTPS to our backend to generate a reply suggestion, then returned to your keyboard.

### What the keyboard does NOT do

- **We do not log your keystrokes.**
- **We do not silently read or transmit text** from apps where you haven't triggered a FlirtIQ action.
- **We do not access secure input fields** — iOS blocks keyboards from reading passwords, credit card numbers, and similar inputs by design.
- **We do not sell any keyboard-derived data.**

### Retention

Text sent through the keyboard is processed to generate your reply and stored only as part of your in-app chat history, which you can clear at any time. We do not retain keyboard-derived data longer than needed for the AI request and your history.

## 4. AI Processing

FlirtIQ uses artificial intelligence to generate reply suggestions, pickup lines, and chat analysis. To do this:

- Text you submit to FlirtIQ is sent to third-party large language model (LLM) providers such as OpenAI.
- These providers process your text to generate a response and return it to us.
- Our agreements with these providers prohibit them from using your data to train their models.
- These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data in transit.

We do not use your chat content to train AI models ourselves.
